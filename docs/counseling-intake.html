Readiness & Consent

Before we begin, please read and acknowledge the following. This protects both you and your counselor.

Pastoral Counseling Acknowledgment

Adam Johns provides pastoral counseling as an ordained minister through the National Association of Christian Ministers (NACM) and Christian Leaders Alliance (CLA). This is not licensed mental health therapy.

Under Virginia Code §54.1-3501(3), ordained ministers are exempt from licensure requirements when providing counseling within the scope of their ministerial duties.

Pastoral counseling is grounded in Scripture, prayer, and faith-based wisdom. If you require licensed mental health services (e.g., diagnosis, medication, trauma therapy), Adam may refer you to appropriate licensed professionals.

Confidentiality is maintained within pastoral scope — consistent with the guidance of Scripture and the ethical standards of NACM/CLA. Mandatory reporting laws apply where required by law.

Confidentiality

What you share in sessions is held in confidence. Information may be shared only in situations involving imminent risk of harm, as required by law, or with your explicit written consent.
