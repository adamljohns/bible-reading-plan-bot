Mentoring vs. Counseling

Mentoring is not counseling. A mentor has walked the road — and walks it with you. A counselor addresses wounds. Both matter. Both are offered here. But they are different offerings and it's important you know which one you need.

Mentoring is about forward movement. It assumes you're basically functional and want to sharpen the blade — to grow as a husband, father, leader, steward, and man of God. If you're carrying deeper wounds, see Counseling.

Adam mentors from experience — not theory. He has fought in every front listed below and has scars and wisdom from each one.
