📋 About This Credential

The NASM Performance Enhancement Specialization (PES) is an advanced athletic performance credential used by coaches across every major professional sport. 100% of NFL teams, 100% of NBA teams, and 100% of MLB teams employ coaches with NASM PES credentials.

The PES curriculum focuses on speed development, agility training, power output, sport-specific conditioning, plyometric progressions, and performance-focused program design. It's built on the CPT foundation and extends it into the realm of athletic coaching.

PES-certified coaches program for the full spectrum of physical performance: from linear speed to multi-directional agility, from force production to reactive strength — everything that separates good athletes from elite ones.

Accreditation: NCCA-accredited — the same standard applied to the CPT
