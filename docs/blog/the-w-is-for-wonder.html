The &#8220;W&#8221; is for Wonder

July 12, 2011 · Men's Ministry

Bible.us/ps119.18 “Open my eyes to behold wondrous things…"

My mind was still active while I was attempting to put into practice a principle of respecting the time of others (i.e. Not emailing or texting them every hour of every day). This and the next post (on Sustainability) I wrote down while I gave everyone a break from my spam (even though I define my SPAM: Spiritual, Personal, Affirming Messages) let me know if you think the below falls into that category.

“I wonder if anyone has wondered this week or last about what happened to the 30MM program…

I honestly have had to force restrain myself from sending group messages as I focus on how wonderful God is, which is what I really believe He's wanted me to do every time I've wanted to send a group message… Until now, although I'm still focusing on Him, I just want to include y'all now.

The point though of this message is not whether or not you wondered why you didn't get messages, the point is this: Does God cause us to stand in wonder? – because He is truly wondrous! (literally: extraordinarily good).

Just wondering… 

Peace,
Adam
