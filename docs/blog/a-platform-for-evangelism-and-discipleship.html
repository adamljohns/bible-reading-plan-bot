A Platform for Evangelism and Discipleship

September 19, 2011 · Men's Ministry

My mentor, one of the 30 Mighty Men on my “Paul List" recently asked me what my lifestyle evangelism platform looked like after he was able to spend the weekend scouting out hunting sites with a few good men in search of future possibilities. Below is my reply as we cross the country in 3 Phrogs from east coast to west…


I'd have to say I see my ideal evangelical platform quite similar to the platform I'm flying now: all weather, amphibious, day/night capable – currently cruising straight and level with a narrow focus and a wide scan, feeling pretty elevated at a medium altitude pulling 85% of my power available keeping 15% in reserves. That still keeps me moving pretty fast without leaving my wingman behind. We're best employed in groups of 2 or 3 for mutual supportability. Plenty of comm capability as long as you don't lose line of sight. Endurance depends on the amount of fuel in the tank, so long trips require a few stops. This platform has stood the test of time although replacements have been attempted. It can sometimes be uncomfortable, but has adequate accommodations… Best part is the capacity to carry 12 fully combat loaded warriors into the fight! Yeah, I guess you could say I like my platform! And I suppose that's why we call it “God's Chariot!"

Peace,
Adam
