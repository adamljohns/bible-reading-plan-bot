A Scriptural Equation

October 06, 2011 · Men's Ministry

Just got some elevation from a 1st Lt from last years TBS Golf company on a video I took of a technique I felt led to put into practice. The technique was to use one of the extra Bibles a Chaplain friend gave me and mark it up with highlighter and dog-ear the pages and leave it in the night stand of the room in which we stand duty.

So in writing him back and wishing him well, I thought of this to add as I prayed that God would direct his steps as he planned his ways: Jeremiah 29:11 + Proverbs 16:9 = Psalm 23:6!!!

Just thought it would be worth sharing with anyone who reads this; a little example of what can happen when we elevate the level of communication — taking something like a mass video text and replying with something as short and simple as: “Oorah MOOP"

Peace,
Adam
