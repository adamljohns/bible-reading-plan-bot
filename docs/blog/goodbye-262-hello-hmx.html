Goodbye 262, Hello HMX

August 02, 2009 · General

Once again, it’s been way to long since I’ve updated this blog, and I must confess, Facebook is much to blame.  However, with the inspiration of my wife and her blogging prowess, as well as some key events in the last few months, I think I have the ammunition to contribute to the blogging world.This first post is simply about leaving one squadron and arriving at another.  I’ve got a couple pictures of my last day at the squadron where I was blessed to receive an award for my 4 1/2 years at HMM-262 from the current 31st MEU Commander, Col Damren (also a former Flying Tiger CO).  Although I share these photos, I do want to add (as I said to my fellow Flying Tigers) it’s not about me.  Nothing I accomplished over my 4 1/2 years at the squadron was done alone, and because of the hard work of those around me I was fortunate enough to be recognized on my way out the door.

I wanted to post these because nobody in my family was able to be present, (Maria and Gideon helping with the pack-up and move out, and the extended family all in the states of course)
