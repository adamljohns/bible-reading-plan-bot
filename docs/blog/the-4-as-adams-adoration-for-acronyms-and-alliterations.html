The 4 A&#8217;s: Adam&#8217;s Adoration for Acronyms &amp; Alliterations

October 21, 2011 · Spiritual Formation

People who know me, know I like acronyms, BUT I DON'T like the KISS acronym. The reason I don't like it is that most people say this one wrong. They say, “Keep It Simple, Stupid," but originally it meant “Keep It Short & Simple."

I don't like being called stupid, so I don't like people calling people stupid, therefore I reject the KISS acronym and propose a new one.
I'll make every effort to use this, and I hope to mention to anyone whenever I hear them talk about the KISS principle. It's 2 more letters, and only one more syllable: “MIM SIS." Kind of like BAMCIS – the Marine's Troop Leading steps, (except a MI instead of a BA, and an S vs. the C…)

Here's my recommendation: to make all communication Meaningful, Interesting, Memorable – Short, Intentional & Simple.
MIM SIS

Well you can make it your own, or reject it vehemently, but please understand my point and stop assuming stupidity.  Like the Scripture says: consider others – as BETTER than yourselves – you don't think you're stupid, do you?

Peace,
Adam

Philippians 2:3 (NLT)
