Time for a check-up

July 26, 2011 · Spiritual Formation

Gideon's getting his school entrance exam for kindergarten today. He's a pro like his little brother Bo as you can see.

Yesterday, I sent a message to the family saying Boaz was back above his birth weight by one ounce: 7 lbs 11 oz now, which is huge! And by HUGE I mean, Holy Undeserved God-given Encouragement, because it was not so with little jaundice Gideon almost 5 years ago. But an answer to prayer has been given with his little brother big time!!!

So, with all these visits to the doctor, I got to thinking: when's the last time I paid a visit to the Great Physician? If it's true that He heals our disease (Ps 103:3) then perhaps we could use a check-up from time to time. And by time to time, I mean: morning time to night time, and every time in-between!

The Doctor is in… Time to see Him!

Peace,
Adam
