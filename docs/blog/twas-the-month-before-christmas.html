&#8217;twas the month before Christmas

November 13, 2011 · General

Got this as a friend, and after a few MOOP modifications, I thought I'd share it on here. From the names included its probably at least 3 or 4 years old, but it's still pretty appropriate for where we are in the current place and time… Enjoy!

Twas the month before Christmas
When all through our land,
Not a Church group was praying, very few who were taking a stand.

Why the PC Police had taken away
The reason for Christmas – no one could say.

The children were told by schools not to sing
About Shepherds and Wise Men or Angels and Kings.

It might hurt people's feelings, some teachers would say;
December 25th is just a ‘ Holiday ‘.

Yet the shoppers were ready with cash, checks and credit
Pushing folks down to the floor just to get it!

CDs from Madonna, an X BOX, or I-Pod
Something was changing, something quite odd!

Retailers promoted Ramadan and Kwanzaa
In hopes to sell books by Franken & Fonda.

As Target was hanging their trees upside-down
At Lowe's the word Christmas – was no where to be found.

In K-Mart and Staples or Penny's and Sears
You won't hear the word Christmas; it won't touch your ears.

“Inclusive," “sensitive," “Di-ver-si-ty"
Are words that were used to intimidate me.

Now Daschle, Now Darden, Now Sharpton, Wolf Blitzen
On Boxer, on Rather, on Kerry, on Clinton!

At the top of the Senate, there arose such a clatter
To eliminate Jesus, in all public matter.

And we spoke not a word, as they took away our freedom.
“You can't speak these things; forget God, we don't need Him."

Although some could still share the hope of their faith,
In the small towns of Texas, teachers taught of God's grace.

But in most cities the True Gift was exchanged and discarded
The reason for the season, had stopped before it started.

So as you celebrate ‘Winter Break' under your ‘Dream Trees'
Sipping your Starbucks, please listen to me.

Choose your words carefully, consider what you say.
Shout MERRY CHRISTMAS,
not Happy Holiday !

For Christ is The Reason' for the Christ-mas Season!

So join together all Christians and please let the world see,
by wishing MERRY CHRISTMAS to everyone you meet!
