July 22, 2007 Update, Almost Done

July 25, 2007 · General

>Here's a picture and an update for the blog so that my running picture isn't the first thing people see. It's probably a larger picture so you might have to resize it.

Anyway, this was about a week and a half ago. My crew was (L to R) AO: LCpl Longhurst CC: LCpl Jordan Me, and CP: 1stLt Ryan Caulder. It was a VIP flight into Baghdad with like 7 planes. Pretty cool flight, unfortunately I don't have any pictures of it, case I was -2. (the second aircraft in the formation)

Things are going well here though, only a few more weeks to go. The ADVON is leaving in less than 2, (that's advanced party) and they'll be getting back to Oki about a week and a half to 2 weeks before the Main Body – that's when I get home. Hopefully it'll be in time for Gideon's B-day on the 16th, but we'll see. We plan on celebrating when I get home anyway. Also right after I get home is Maria and my 4th year Anniversary, which I'm very glad I'll be home for. Many of my friends here have had to spend their anniversary away from their wife.

The newest News Letter (Tiger Tales) will be out in a week or two, and it will be the last one for a little while. I still have to write my newsletter input, so I'll save some words for that.

Again, thanks for all your prayers, letters, calls, e-mails, and instant messages, it's good to keep in touch. Hopefully I'll see all of you sometime soon.

Peace,
Adam
