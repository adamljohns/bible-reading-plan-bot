The Four Pillars

Each letter represents a pillar of our ministry calling.

U.
Uniting

Bringing men together across denominations, backgrounds, and walks of life. We believe the body of Christ is strongest when brothers lock shields — not when they build walls between their churches.

S.
Serving

Leading by example through selfless service — to our families, our churches, and our communities. A man who serves like Christ transforms everything he touches.

M.
Mentoring

Older men investing in younger men. Experienced fathers guiding new fathers. Seasoned leaders pouring into emerging ones. Titus 2 in action — the ancient model of passing the torch.

C.
Counseling

Biblical counseling for men navigating hard seasons — marriage struggles, identity crises, grief, addiction recovery, and spiritual warfare. Not therapy. Brotherhood with truth.

About the Founder

U.S.M.C. Ministries was founded by Adam "MOOP" Johns, a Marine Corps veteran, lay minister, and SYMBIS & PREPARE/ENRICH certified facilitator. MOOP stands for Marine On Own Program — a call sign earned in service that now represents a life fully surrendered to Christ's program.

After 20+ years of military service, Adam answered the call to help men lead their families with the same discipline, intentionality, and sacrifice they bring to their professional lives — but anchored in Scripture and empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Member of Christian Leaders Alliance · National Association of Christian Ministers · CBMC · SYMBIS · PREPARE/ENRICH
