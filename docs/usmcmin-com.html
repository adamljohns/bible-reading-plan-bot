Background

Who Is Moop?

I'm Adam "MOOP" Johns — Marine Corps veteran, husband to Maria, and father to Gideon, Boaz, and Shiloh. My call sign stuck because I've always been the one who figures things out. That same drive shapes everything I do: training, ministry, consulting, and family.

After serving in the Corps, I answered a different call — to equip men to lead with integrity, faith, and purpose. That led to USMC Ministries, men's discipleship work, and a commitment to building brothers who can carry the weight of their families and their communities.

Today I work as a fit20 trainer and Studio Coordinator, run C5iSR LLC (Christ-centered consulting for the digital economy), and stay active in men's ministry, Bible teaching, and spiritual formation. Everything under one mission: serve well.
