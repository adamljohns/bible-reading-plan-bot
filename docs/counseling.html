What Counseling Addresses

Counseling addresses what mentoring cannot — the wounds, the crises, the relational fractures that require more than a sharp iron. This is structured, confidential, and rooted in Scripture and certified frameworks.

This is not a program. It is a relationship — a structured, intentional conversation between a man carrying something heavy and a minister equipped and willing to help carry it. It's pastoral. It's biblical. And it's confidential.

If you're not sure whether you need mentoring or counseling, book a consultation — together we'll figure out which door opens what you need. See also: Mentoring →

Types of Counseling Offered

01
Pre-Marital Counseling
For couples preparing for marriage. Using the SYMBIS (Save Your Marriage Before It Starts) framework — a proven, research-based approach to building a foundation before the ceremony.
SYMBIS Certified → symbis.com
02
Marriage Counseling & Enrichment
For couples in struggle or wanting to grow stronger. Using the PREPARE/ENRICH certified framework — one of the most widely used and researched marriage assessment tools available.
PREPARE/ENRICH Certified → prepare-enrich.com
03
Men in Crisis
Confidential and non-judgmental. For men facing addiction, career collapse, identity fracture, relational breakdown, or moral failure. No platitudes. No condemnation. Just truth, presence, and a path forward.
04
Military Transition Counseling
Specific to veterans navigating the loss of identity, mission, structure, and brotherhood after service. Adam walked this road. He knows what no one tells you before you take off the uniform for the last time.
05
Grief and Loss
Loss of purpose, loss of relationships, loss of loved ones. Grief is not a weakness — it is proof that something mattered. This space holds that grief without rushing past it.

If You're in a Dark Place

You are not alone. You have not gone too far. Help is available right now.

  • Veterans Crisis Line: 1-800-273-8255, press 1 — 24/7 for veterans and their families
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 — available 24/7
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741 — free, confidential, 24/7
