The Town

Colonial & Civil War
Historical, Charming, Timeless

Fredericksburg, Virginia is full of America's most formative history, from the Revolutionary War to the Civil War. Much of the town is preserved with tours, plaques, and walks that bring to life all that took place in this remarkable city.

The city sits on the Rappahannock River and is home to four Civil War battle sites: Fredericksburg, Chancellorsville, Wilderness, and Spotsylvania.

Catch the Trolley tour around town to get an extensive overview of the city's history — it brings to life every corner and block of Fredericksburg!

Within walking distance of our stays you'll find award-winning restaurants, boutique shopping, riverfront trails, art galleries, and one of the most charming Main Streets on the East Coast.
